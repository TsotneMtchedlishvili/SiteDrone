FIREWATCHER

Firewatcher is a firefighting drone project, aimed at eliminating small scale, class A, natural, urban or suburban fires. It's primary function is to carry a concentrated, light weightload of fire retardant substance, contained in a spherical missle. The drone's design seeks to maximize cost efficiency and mobility and allow for swift responses to fire sources before they have a chance to spread, this way not only can countermeasures be deployed immediately but it can also prove to be a lot cheaper as well. Needless to say that if the project is developed further, the drone could possibly assist in subversion of traffic in emergency situations.
